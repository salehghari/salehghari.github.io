trigonometric functions from 0 to 90 degrees

In this app, you can find any trigonometric function from 0 to 90 degrees with a click! As you can see there are some numbers at the top and the left of the screen that are approximately one centimeter apart. So if you click somewhere on the screen that is 2 cm long and 2 cm wide, the formed angle will be 45 degrees and sine, cosine, tangent and cotangent of 45 degrees will appear in the Result section.
